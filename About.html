About Mac N Eats

This project was created by Grace, Ethan, Maria, Katya, and Deeya as part of our demo. Mac N-Eats is a sustainable food access platform designed specifically for McMaster students.

  • Many on-campus food vendors discard perfectly good food that doesn't sell by the end of the day.
  • At the same time, numerous McMaster students—especially those relying on OSAP—struggle with food insecurity.
  • Mac N-Eats provides discounted, unsold fresh food from campus vendors through a mobile app and website.
  • Unlike apps like "Too Good To Go," eligibility is based on student financial need, increasing accessibility.
  • Vendors upload surplus food, triggering notifications for eligible students to purchase items at reduced prices.
  • Service operates Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, with pickups located inside participating food vendors.
  • We promote the service through social media to ensure students in need know where to find support.
